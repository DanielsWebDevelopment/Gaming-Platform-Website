What is player of the week

In the realm of competitive gaming, the Player of the Week stands as a beacon of exceptional skill and unrivaled performance. This prestigious title is awarded to an individual who has exhibited unparalleled prowess, strategic brilliance, and an unwavering dedication to their craft.
